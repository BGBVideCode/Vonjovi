About BGB

Rare isn't just a category for us.

It's a way of thinking, behaving and delivering efficiently on the needs of patients and the HCPs who treat them. To us, a disease that progresses this quickly needs equally progressive thinking and partnerships.

Grounded in science. Driven by human truth. Fueled by exceptional creativity.

This is our commitment to Sobi Vonjo, HCPs, and their patients. Rare, in every way, shape and form.
